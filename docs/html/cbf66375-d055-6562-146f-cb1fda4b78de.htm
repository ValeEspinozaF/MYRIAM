      InputParametersANG_ROT Property

      Array of three rotation angles (in degrees) used to improve the generation of the torque-variation pole confidence contours, since these can be inaccurate when the bulk of the torque-variation ensemble is close to any of the polar regions. The angles instruct MYRIAM to perform the three elemental rotations that seek to center and horizontally flatten the torque-variation ensemble on 0degN--0degE, where the contouring is more accurate.
